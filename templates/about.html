About Us

The Indian Legal System caters to a population of nearly 135 crores, and follows the Common Law system, which means judgments are primarily based on set precedents. Our product, Needhi Legal Search aims to leverage technology to increase accessibility into the world of law by replacing the jargon-laden case document archive by a specialised legal search engine, which returns relevant documents, with accompanying data visualisations, that help you navigate easily to case files you are looking for!

Referencing and researching past judgments is an important step in filing, fighting, or deciding a case. Needhi Legal Search eliminates the need of sifting physically through the millions of documents available based on established law and legal proceedings and presents the users the top documents for any query they type!
